# Grammar Checker Skill

## Description
The Grammar Checker skill focuses specifically on detecting and correcting grammatical issues common in academic writing.

## Capabilities

### 1. Subject-Verb Agreement
- Identify mismatches between subjects and verbs
- Handle complex sentences with multiple clauses
- Check agreement in passive constructions

### 2. Tense Consistency
- Verify consistent use of tenses throughout document
- Identify inappropriate tense shifts
- Ensure proper tense usage in academic contexts

### 3. Article Usage
- Correct use of definite article (the)
- Correct use of indefinite articles (a/an)
- Handle article usage with proper nouns, plurals, and uncountable nouns

### 4. Preposition Errors
- Identify incorrect preposition choices
- Fix common preposition mistakes in academic phrases
- Ensure proper prepositional phrases

### 5. Sentence Structure Analysis
- Identify overly long or complex sentences
- Flag run-on sentences and fragments
- Suggest sentence splitting for readability

### 6. Vague Expression Detection
- Identify vague or imprecise language
- Suggest more specific terminology
- Improve clarity of expressions

## Input Requirements
- Plain text or document content
- Optional: specific grammar focus areas

## Output
- Corrected text with grammar issues fixed
- List of changes with explanations
- Suggestions for avoiding common errors

## Configuration Options
- `strictness`: lenient | standard | strict
- `focus_areas`: comma-separated list of areas to check
- `explain_changes`: true | false
